Loan Operator

Valley Green Bank has operated as a top-performing community bank in the Philadelphia region since 2005. Customer service-driven business has allowed Valley Green Bank to make effective loans to small businesses and real estate developers that would otherwise go unfunded. Valley Green Bank has not only been the driving force behind economic growth in the Philadelphia region, but has grown to 5 offices and has become ranked as one of the top 10 performing small banks in the nation. As of January 2015, Valley Green Bank is now a division of Univest Bank and Trust Co., a publicly traded community bank that seeks to further the impact Valley Green Bank has had on the Philadelphia Community. As a loan operator for Valley Green Bank, I helped facilitate key communications between loan officers (lenders), their clients, and insurance companies. I managed regulatory compliance, ensuring that all leveraged properties were insured. Customer service played a major role in my tasks, as I dealt with both insurance providers and clients personally to streamline the transfer of information. Additional responsibilities included loan documentation filing and teller training.

  • Client: Valley Green Bank
  • Date: Summer 2012, 2013
  • Service: Customer Service
